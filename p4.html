Project #4 Experimental Clock

Description

an experimental clock visually showing the portion of time has passed of a day. the background color (outside of the window) can be changed according to day and night, and interaction with the clock is enabled by allowing people to use mouse hang over the clok to change the color or click to see exact number of the time.

Reflection

the outcome is actually a little bit different from what I was expecting. I tried a lot but failed to make the "second" section of clock automatically change color everytime that passed to another minute. But instead I did some other experiments to try to make the clock fun. Hope they work.
