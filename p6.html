Project #6 : Optical Illusion

Description

A profile of Marilyn Monroe in pixel style, pixels change following the movement of the mouse. The work reflects how the being of a celebrity is always constructed by viewers ambiguously but at the same time distorted into a symbol, especially in the cyber world
